Common questions

Before you book a call.

Do you need access to our AWS account?

For audits, we request read-only IAM access so we can analyse your infrastructure and cost data. For deployments, we'll need write access to the target account. We can work within your existing security policies and are happy to use temporary credentials or assume-role setups.

What if we don't use Kubernetes?

That's completely fine — and honestly, many of our clients shouldn't be using Kubernetes. We work with ECS, EC2-based deployments, serverless, and hybrid setups. If a simpler platform fits your needs better, we'll recommend it.

How quickly can you start?

Troubleshooting engagements can typically start within 24–48 hours. Deployments and audits usually kick off within a week of the discovery call, depending on scheduling and access provisioning.

What does the money-back guarantee cover?

If our Architecture Efficiency Audit doesn't identify any actionable cost savings or architecture improvements, we refund the full engagement fee minus a $100 processing fee.

Do you offer ongoing support after an engagement?

Yes. After any engagement, we offer optional retainer-based support for questions, incident response, and infrastructure changes. Many clients start with a deployment or audit and then move to a lightweight monthly retainer.

Will we be locked in to CloudForge after delivery?

No. Everything we build is yours — Terraform repos, documentation, runbooks, monitoring dashboards. You can maintain it yourself, hand it to another team, or bring us back. Zero vendor lock-in.

Not sure which service you need?

Book a free discovery call and we'll figure it out together. No pressure, no sales pitch — just a technical conversation about your infrastructure.
