About CloudForge

We've been on the other side
of the infrastructure problem.

CloudForge was built by two former AWS engineers who spent years working alongside teams of every size — helping them troubleshoot production issues, establish best practices, optimise cloud spend, and build infrastructure they could actually maintain and replicate. We started this practice to bring that same guidance directly to the organisations that need it most.

The problem we kept seeing

During our time at AWS, we worked with engineering teams of every size — from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. The pattern was remarkably consistent: organisations would adopt Kubernetes because it was the industry standard, without fully accounting for the operational burden that comes with it.

Engineers who were hired to build product features found themselves deep in cluster troubleshooting instead — debugging pod failures, chasing networking issues, and deciphering YAML configs with little to no Kubernetes experience. Many were learning on the fly, piecing together answers from Stack Overflow at 2am during production incidents. The cognitive load of managing a platform they never signed up to own was burning teams out, slowing delivery, and driving cloud costs through the roof.

We built CloudForge to give these teams something they couldn't easily find: honest, experienced infrastructure guidance from people who've seen what works and what doesn't — at every scale.

✓ Simplicity over sophistication

✓ You own everything

✓ Straight talk
